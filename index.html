About Us

Maxil Agro Industries

Maxil Agro Industries is world’s renowned Manufacturer & Exporter of Basmati & Non-Basmati Rice from District Karnal, State Haryana in Country India. Our factory and processing unit established in the year 2009 in City Taraori of District Karnal, Haryana (India). We are engaged in Milling, Processing & Supplying of superior and finest, export quality Basmati Rice in the form of Creamy Sella / White Sella (Parboiled Rice), Golden Sella, Steam & Raw of all varieties to all across the globe. Maxil Agro Industries is a USFDA and SFDA certified manufacturing unit having ISO 9001:2015, ISO 22000:2018, HACCP, GMP, HALAL, KOSHER & FSSAI certifications as well in production & supply, which ensures the high-quality products to our clients.

Our Certification

 

  • Carbs

    Carbs in rice are mainly in the form of starch, accounting for up to 90% of the total dry weight.

  • Fiber

    The bran is mainly composed of insoluble fibers, such as hemicellulose, and contains virtually no soluble fiber.

  • Vitamins

    Many vitamins and minerals are concentrated in the bran and germ, which are components of rice.

  • Plant Compound

    A number of plant compounds are found in rice, which are linked with potential health benefits.

  • Copper

    Often found in whole grains, copper is low in the Western diet. Poor copper status may have adverse effects on heart health.

  • Niacin

    Also known as vitamin B3, niacin in rice is mostly in the form of nicotinic acid.
